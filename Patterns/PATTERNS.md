Patterns are a great way to master nested loops and logic building. To identify the logic behind any pattern, follow these three simple steps:

1. **Count the lines**: How many rows does the pattern have? (Outer loop)
2. **Identify the columns**: For each row, how many items (stars, numbers, characters) are printed? (Inner loop)
3. **Determine the content**: What exactly needs to be printed? (The value inside the inner loop)

## Available Pattern Programs

- **Inverted Star Pattern**: [InvertedStarPattern.java](./InvertedStarPattern.java) - Prints an inverted star triangle.
